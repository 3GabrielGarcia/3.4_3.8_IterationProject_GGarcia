What Do We Do?

Ididikit is a design company that offers customers the opportunity to commission custom designs, ranging from Vinyl Designs, Heat Prints, and Laser Engraving. Ididikit designs are able to be put on many different products such as T-shirts, Cutting Boards, Mugs, Bottles, tote bags, and much more.

Our Products

What started as making personalized items for friends soon grew into a full-fledged small business. Over time, the shop expanded into laser engraved items, taking on more intricate and meaningful products that bring personal touches to everyday items and special occasions.

Our Company History

Ididikit was born during the pandemic from a simple hobby that quickly turned into something more. After being gifted a Cricut machine, I saw an opportunity to combine my design background with my love for creating personalised gifts and products.
